The Weaponry of Silence

Acoustic Harassment

The use of V2K (Voice to Skull), infrasound, or high-frequency emitters to cause sleep deprivation, disorientation, and physical distress without leaving physical evidence.

Hostile Surveillance

Abusers utilize hidden cameras, compromised smart-home devices, and GPS trackers to ensure the victim feels watched in every "safe" space.

Narcissistic Tech Abuse

Leveraging administrative access to lock accounts, manipulate thermostat/lighting, and spoofing messages to gaslight victims into doubting their reality.

The Discrediting Trap

Tactics are often chosen specifically to make the victim appear "unreliable" to authorities and family.

Gay & LGBTQ+ Men

Exploiting social stigmas or professional environments to threaten "outing" or using homophobic tropes to frame the victim as hyper-sexualized or unstable.

Recovery & Substance Use

Abusers track those with a history of drug use to frame tech-harassment as "drug-induced paranoia," ensuring police ignore legitimate reports of stalking.

Survivors of Abuse

Targeting those already traumatized to trigger "Hyper-vigilance," which the abuser then points to as evidence of mental instability to colleagues and friends.

The Goal of Discrediting: By targeting vulnerable identities, the abuser creates a "Character Assassination" shield. When the victim asks for help, the abuser points to their identity or history to invalidate their claims.

Protecting the Perimeter

For Victims

  • Safe Device: Access help from a library or a new "clean" burner phone.
  • Log Frequencies: Use sound-meter apps to record unusual decibel spikes.
  • 2FA: Move all security to App-based authentication, not SMS.

For Allies

  • Believe them: Tech abuse is designed to sound "crazy." Listen without judgment.
  • Observe: Note if the person is suddenly isolated or their devices act strangely.
  • Secure Space: Offer your home as a "tech-free" sanctuary for them to rest.

Emergency Resources

If you are in immediate physical danger, contact local emergency services from a safe phone.
